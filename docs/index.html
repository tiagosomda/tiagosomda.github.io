Engineer. Explorer. Happy.
Tiago Sommer Damasceno, a Senior Software Engineer at Microsoft and Board Member at GOKiC, is passionate about teaching computer science via game development. He was born and raised in Brazil, where a teacher introduced him to game development. As a result, he became curious about programming which took him to computer science and software engineering. Tiago knows that positive change can come from sparking people's curiosity! Thus, he is committed to teaching science, technology, engineering and mathematics to kids!
