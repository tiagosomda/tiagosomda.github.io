Maker." +++ Tiago Sommer Damasceno is a passionate software developer who is currently a Senior Gameplay Engineer at Ubisoft, where he has helped bring Assassin's Creed Nexus VR to life. He is now helping bring some exciting stuff into XDefiant!

Previously, he worked at Microsoft as a Senior Software Engineer where he released many internal and external products such as Windows and Test Base for Microsoft 365.

A few domains that he is interested in:

XR (eXtended Reality):
This domain is the tech I am most passionated about it. There is still so much to explore and create in this domain.

Decentralization:
My favorite initiatives are Mastodon, Solid.mit.edu / Inrupt.
To me, a great tech for decentralization is Blockchain. I think it can be used for very robust decentralized systems. However, as you can see with Mastodon, Solid, and Inrupt, not everything needs to be on a blockchain.

Artificial Intelligence:
AI is changing the world. We need to make sure that we create AIs in an ethical and open way so that everyone can benefit from the power that this technology can bring. Inevitably, there will be private AI solutions, but like Linux, the foundation of AI should be open to all.
